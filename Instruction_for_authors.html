Submission Guidelines for Authors

General Submission Information

Prospective authors are invited to submit original technical papers for publication in the AIHC 2027. All papers should adhere to the following guidelines:

Key Requirements:

  • All conference submissions should be written in English.
  • Maximum paper length is six (6) printed pages (10-point font), including figures and tables.
  • Review is single blind. Papers having poor quality and/or high similarity index will be desk rejected (without review).
  • All the co-authors must be listed with affiliation details in the initial submission. No further authors can be added for selected papers.
  • All the registered papers must be presented at the conference.

Template and Format

  • LaTeX is the preferred method to prepare your manuscript. Please use the standard LaTeX AIHC conference template.
  • For Microsoft Word, please use the most current version of the AIHC conference template, which will help reduce word-to-pdf conversion issues.

Camera-Ready Instructions

  • Authors who receive final acceptance after paper revision can only register for the conference. The authors will also be expected to submit a final version of the manuscript that will be added to AIHC proceedings as per the conference template.
  • Please upload camera-ready paper and an eCopy transfer file in **Microsoft CMT**.
  • Please make sure that the camera-ready paper has been generated by the template provided to you (Contains AIHC copyright notice on the first page bottom left, and the conference name as the footer on the rest of the pages) and verified with the PDF Express website.
